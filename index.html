About me

I am a passionate graphic designer with a strong foundation in visual communication, branding, and digital design. Over the past 5years, I’ve worked on a variety of projects including logo design, social media content, posters, marketing materials, and UI mockups. My design style is clean, modern, and user-focused, with attention to detail and consistency. Currently, I am expanding my skill set as an upcoming web developer, learning front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and exploring responsive design, accessibility. My goal is to bridge the gap between design and development—creating websites that are not only visually engaging but also functional and user-friendly. This portfolio highlights selected works that reflect both my design capabilities and my growing technical skills in web development.

My Projects

Logos

This collection showcases a logo design created for a brand. Each logo is crafted with a focus on simplicity, versatility, and brand identity, ensuring it communicates the essence of the business it represents. From modern and minimalistic marks to more expressive and illustrative styles, these designs reflect a deep understanding of typography, color theory, and symbolism. My process involves thorough research, sketching, and digital refinement to deliver logos that are both memorable and adaptable across platforms. These logos are more than visuals—they are the foundation of a brand’s visual identity, designed to leave a lasting impression.

Social Media Posts

This collection features a variety of social media post designs created to engage audiences and promote brands effectively. Each design is tailored to fit the unique style and tone of the platform it’s intended for, whether it’s Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn. The posts incorporate eye-catching visuals, compelling typography, and strategic use of color to capture attention and convey messages clearly. My approach involves understanding the target audience, current trends, and the brand’s voice to create content that resonates and encourages interaction. These social media posts are designed not only to look great but also to drive engagement and foster community around the brand.

Posters

This collection showcases a series of poster designs created for various events, promotions, and campaigns. Each poster is designed with a focus on visual impact, clarity of message, and aesthetic appeal. The designs utilize bold typography, striking imagery, and thoughtful layout to ensure that the key information stands out and captures the viewer’s attention. My process involves understanding the purpose of the poster, the target audience, and the context in which it will be displayed to create effective and memorable designs. These posters are more than just visuals; they are powerful communication tools designed to inform, persuade, and inspire action.
